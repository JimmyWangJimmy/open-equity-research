# Financial and research disclaimer

Open Equity Research is an educational and research tool. It does not provide
personalized investment, legal, accounting, or tax advice. It does not know a
user's objectives, financial situation, risk tolerance, constraints, or tax
position. Outputs are not offers, solicitations, recommendations, or guarantees
of future performance.

The project deliberately excludes broker connectivity and order execution.
Financial statements, XBRL tags, derived metrics, document parsers, language
models, valuation assumptions, and third-party inputs can all be incomplete or
wrong. Users must verify material facts against original filings and obtain
qualified professional advice where appropriate.

Historical performance and historical financial evidence do not predict future
returns. Investing can result in partial or total loss of capital.
