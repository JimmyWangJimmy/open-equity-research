# Roadmap

## 0.2 — Filing intelligence

- Filing-to-filing diff engine for 10-K, 10-Q, and 8-K.
- More robust section extraction and inline-XBRL handling.
- Custom-tag mapping diagnostics.
- Evidence links to exact filing fragments, not only filing index pages.
- Insider Form 4 and beneficial-ownership modules.

## 0.3 — Point-in-time research

- Provider-neutral market-data interface with timestamps and license metadata.
- Historical “what was knowable then?” snapshots.
- Earnings-call and investor-presentation connectors with provenance.
- News/event evidence with publication and event times separated.
- Staleness and contradiction detection across sources.

## 0.4 — Evaluation

- A benchmark of research questions with expert-labeled evidence support.
- Single-agent versus role-separated-agent comparisons.
- Cost, latency, unsupported-claim, citation-correctness, and revision metrics.
- Walk-forward backtesting harness with delistings, corporate actions, fees, slippage, and data-availability controls.

## 0.5 — Portfolio research

- Watchlists and coverage ownership.
- Thesis-change monitoring.
- Exposure, concentration, correlation, liquidity, and scenario analysis.
- Human approval records for any portfolio-level decision.

## Explicit non-goals

- Guaranteed-return claims.
- Hidden auto-trading.
- Credential storage for brokers.
- Personalized suitability decisions without a regulated and appropriately supervised context.
